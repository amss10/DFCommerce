## 🚀 How to Use Your Store

### Browse Products
1. Open **http://localhost:8000**
2. Scroll down to see 6 category carousels
3. Each carousel shows 4-5 items
4. Click **❮** or **❯** to scroll through products
5. Click **"See All >"** to view entire category

### Search for Products
1. Use the search bar at the top
2. Type product name (e.g., "headphones")
3. Press Enter or click Search
4. View results in grid format
5. Click Reset to go back to carousels

### Filter by Category
1. Use the category dropdown
2. Select a category
3. Click Apply
4. View all products in that category
5. Click Reset to see all categories again

### Add to Cart
1. Click "Add to Cart" on any product
2. Item appears in Shopping Cart sidebar
3. See updated total price
4. Click "Proceed to Checkout"
5. Complete your order
